A Happy Ever After

Wondering why people still look for soul mates. . Is it because they hope that theirs will be a different case, wanting to taste the waters of love, the pressure from the outside world to have one, the fear of being lonely or is it for security purposes. . Wedding bells ring every weekend, vows are made, exclusive million dollar rings are exchanged as a sign, love in the air, smiles, mirth and glee fills the atmosphere, kisses all the way to honeymoon, Paris, Bahamas, you name it. . What happens thereafter?

The same tongue that uttered sweet words, ‘I love you’,’ I’ll never leave you’ , being one of them, ends up verbally abusing you or even worse bruising your heart and well-being by saying, ‘I don’t know why I even married you, I regret it’, piercing the soul. . That tongue that rolled up in your mouth as you kissed and made out, expressing your love on a deeper level, ends up spitting on you, a sign of being worthless in their eyes, even pity doesn’t count, no slightest emotion whatsoever left. . The same fingers that brushed through your hair, touched you in intimacy, made you fall for them even more, chemistry it was, end up throwing glasses at you, poisoning your food or drinks, they wish you dead or gone for good, slap that same face they once adores and couldn’t stop staring at even blows to be worse. . That same arm that went around your waist, supported you during hikes, you fell asleep on, made you feel safe and protected (ladies can testify to this), the arm that provided the warm shoulder to cry on and made you feel cared for turns cold, its thrown in the air at your face, he/she doesn’t care at all anymore, no feeling of security no more. . Those same eyes that looked deep in yours, watched you fall in a deep slumber, turn bitter, they no longer notice when something troubles you, the tears shed for them are empty. .That same voice that whispered in your ear, the one that sang to you or at least tried to sing harmoniously, becomes loud and shouts at you, anger overrules and fights are picked up just so he/she can storm out and go wherever he/she desires. . A turn of events, what went wrong? Love turned sour or was it pretence? Marriages once based on the foundation of love turn into two people living as roommates, not the same matrimonial bed, the kids are what are keeping them together, once they grow old enough to understand, divorce procedures begin. . A happily ever after becomes a weary one. . By now you may think I’m a sadist, welcome to the real world of promises not lived up to, void words and broken vows. . This shouldn’t keep one from running away from love. . I’m one of those people that still believe in true love despite all. . I choose to work things out with whoever I’ll walk down the aisle with, give him my best shot and be the submissive wife, he’s the King of it all and I his Queen, love and cherish through thick and thin, start from the scratch if so, till he achieves what he wants and know who he is, but at the same time strive towards my ambitions. . Till then, I’m giving everyone I have an equal chance to prove they are worth the future effort. . Meanwhile, all those that are up to the ‘challenge’ can drop their applications and CVs in my email (laughs), just kidding. .

Choose to be different and be optimistic when it comes to love. . I know mine will be a Cinderella story with a happily ever after  Love, Jenny.
